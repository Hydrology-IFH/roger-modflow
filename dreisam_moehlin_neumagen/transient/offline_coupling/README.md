# Fissured and porous Aquifer of the Dreisam-Moehlin-Neumagen catchment

Simulation of the soil water and groundwater of the fissured and porous aquifer of the Dreisam-Moehlin-Neumagen catchment (Germany) using MODFLOW6 coupled with RoGeR. The two models can be coupled offline (i.e. simulations are performed sequentially)
